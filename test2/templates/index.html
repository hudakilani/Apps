OUR SERVICES

Web Development

Create a user-friendly interface which will help Banks with clear and easy-to-interpret visual data.

Predicting The Future

Predict probability of a current customer to leave the bank ranging from 0 to 1 where 0 will denote that customer will never leave and 1 will denote that customer is definitely going to leave the bank.

Help You Analysing Your Data

Provide quick & on-demand data of top percentage of people likely to leave . Enables user to streamline their focus on required number of people . However choosing to predict for any customer from the data is always an option .

Providing All The Details In Graphs

Show all details , prediction of probability of a customer leaving the bank along with its graph. Also show probability of different reasons of leaving for each customer and display easy-to-interpret pie-charts demonstrating each reason for in-depth analysis . This pie-chart graph shows that if probability of churn becomes true and customer really leaves, then what factors will contribute to his/her leaving the organization, i.e. , reasons for leaving and it will show probability of each reason contributing to his/her withdrawal from services of organization.
